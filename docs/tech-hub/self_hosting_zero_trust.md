# Accessing a Self-Hosted Instance

If your organisation runs a self-hosted Open Chat Studio instance, your administrator
may have set up Cloudflare Zero Trust to control access. This means you will need
the Cloudflare WARP client installed and connected before you can reach the app.

Ask your administrator for:

- The app hostname (e.g. `https://ocs.your-org`)
- Your organisation's Cloudflare team name

Once you have those, follow the steps your administrator sends you to enrol your
device and connect.
